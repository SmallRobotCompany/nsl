Farming with Robots

Meet sustainable farming robots Tom, Dick and Harry! Small Robot Company is reimagining farming to feed the world - while regenerating the planet.

Feeding 9 billion people sustainably by 2050 is one of the biggest challenges facing mankind today. We’ll need to increase available food by a massive 70% - but farming methods today place too heavy a burden on our environment.

Small robots have a dream to save the world! We’re working with farmers on our mission to sustainable Per Plant Farming - and better than Net Zero.

Meet our multisensory robots! They can see, hear, smell, taste, and touch: why? And transform soil into a carbon sink. Watch weed zapping - with zero chemicals! See the future of farming, in action, today.
